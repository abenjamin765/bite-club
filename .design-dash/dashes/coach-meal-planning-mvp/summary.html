Problem & Opportunity

Clients in coach-led nutrition programs are stuck re-logging meals in open-ended trackers, producing incomplete data and leaving coaches without clear adherence signal. The MVP makes the approved weekly plan the source of truth and limits daily work to delta check-ins (Followed / Modified / Skipped), with coaches reviewing exceptions via a daily digest.

Why this matters

This problem statement defines the hypothesis being tested. If the intervention does not measurably improve user outcomes, the hypothesis is wrong and the dash re-enters at the appropriate phase.

Product Requirements

Context

Greenfield mobile-first PWA for coach-led nutrition: weekly planning, coach approval, delta daily check-ins, and exception-based coach digest. Soft launch ~12 users. Built with vanilla HTML/CSS/JS on free-tier services.

Goals

  • Daily check-in completion ≥60% (M-002)
  • Median check-in duration ≤3 minutes (M-003)
  • Full loop: plan → approve → check-in → digest (SC-4)
  • Private coach-client visibility only (SC-5)

Non-goals

  • In-app coach comments (deferred)
  • Public group feed, leaderboards, AI meal generation
  • Barcode scanning (Phase 2)

Selected concept

Guided Day Wizard with reuse accelerators (copy last week, add from saved). Selected over Week Grid and Reuse-First — see concepts.md.

IA summary

  • Client home: Daily Check-In hub
  • Planning: day-by-day wizard
  • Coach: Group dashboard + generated daily digest

Account

An Account is the identity record for every person in the system — either a Client or a Coach. Accounts belong to a [Group](group.md) and own [Weekly Meal Plans](weekly-meal-plan.md), [Daily Check-Ins](daily-check-in.md), and [Saved Meals](saved-meal.md).

Full guide →

Group

A **Group** is a coach-owned container that organizes a set of client Accounts into a nutrition program, providing the coach with a unified view of plan status and daily adherence.

Full guide →

Weekly Meal Plan

A **Weekly Meal Plan** is a client's structured record of planned meals, ingredients, and macros for a specific calendar week, which must be approved by their coach before it becomes the basis for daily adherence check-ins.

Full guide →

Day Plan

A **Day Plan** is a single day's planned meals within a Weekly Meal Plan, aggregating the nutritional totals of all planned [Meals](meal.

Full guide →

Meal

A **Meal** is a named eating occasion — breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack, or other — that belongs to one [Day Plan](day-plan.

Full guide →

Ingredient

An Ingredient is the atomic nutrition unit within a [Meal](meal.md), carrying the quantity, unit, and macro values that roll up to meal, day, and week totals. Entered manually or via USDA / Open Food Facts lookup.

Key Decisions

  • Standard tier; solo dash for soft launch (~12 users)
  • Client hub object: Daily Check-In (today-centric home)
  • Adherence statuses: Followed / Modified / Skipped only
  • Coach comments deferred — feedback outside app for MVP
  • Nutrition entry: manual + optional USDA/Open Food Facts lookup
  • Stack: vanilla HTML/CSS/JS on free-tier infrastructure
  • Concept B selected: Guided Day Wizard with reuse accelerators
  • Daily Group Digest modeled as generated Group view (not persisted object)
  • 9 object guides written to library/objects/
