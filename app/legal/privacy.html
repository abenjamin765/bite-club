Privacy at Bite Club

Pilot privacy summary. This document is under legal review and will be finalized before Bite Club is offered beyond the soft-launch pilot.

What we collect

  • Account details: name, email, role, and time zone.
  • Your group membership within your coach's program.
  • Weekly meal plans, ingredients, and macros you enter.
  • Daily check-in statuses and any deviation notes you add.

Who can see it

Your plans and check-ins are private to you and your assigned coach. Other clients cannot see your data, and you cannot see theirs. Coaches see only the clients in their own group.

How it's used

Your data is used to plan your week, let your coach review and approve plans, and summarize daily adherence for your coach. We do not sell your data and there is no advertising or behavioral tracking.

Third parties

Optional food lookups may use USDA FoodData Central or Open Food Facts. Authentication and storage are provided by Supabase.

Your controls

You can request access to or deletion of your account data by contacting your coach or the program administrator during the pilot.
